8 /* A base class for bucket-based shrink strategies.
9 
10  A bucket-based strategy partitions the states into an ordered
11  vector of buckets, from low to high priority, and then abstracts
12  them to a given target size according to the following rules:
13 
14  Repeat until we respect the target size:
